### TfC Right Exists

Does this agreement grant the customer a right to terminate without cause (termination for convenience, at will, or in sole discretion)? State "Yes" or "No". Provide the section reference and page number.

### TfC Details

If a termination-for-convenience right exists, specify: (1) who holds the right, (2) the notice period required, (3) any termination fee or penalty, (4) wind-down obligations. Provide the section reference and page number.

### Mutual or One-Sided

Is the termination-for-convenience right mutual (both parties) or one-sided (customer only)? Provide the section reference and page number.
