## Additional Focus Areas

- contract assignability and anti-assignment provisions
- change-of-control vs assignment consent triggers
- transferability of licenses, permits, and key vendor relationships
- retained vs excluded liabilities

## Additional Instructions

This is an asset purchase, not a stock deal. Consent-to-assign and
non-transferable rights are first-order concerns: a contract that cannot
be assigned without counterparty consent is at risk of being lost on close.
