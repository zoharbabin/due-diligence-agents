# Letter of Intent

**From:** Company A
**To:** Company B
**Date:** April 10, 2024

Dear Company B,

This letter confirms our mutual interest in exploring a potential business
relationship. We look forward to discussing terms in detail.

within 90 days of this letter.

for the confidentiality provisions below.

contents of these discussions to third parties for a period of 12 months.

Sincerely,
Company A
