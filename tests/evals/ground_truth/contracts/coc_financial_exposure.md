# Enterprise Platform License Agreement — Company A / Company C

**Contract Reference:** EPLA-2023-042
**Effective Date:** July 1, 2023
**Term:** 36 months
**Parties:** Company A ("Licensor"), Company C ("Licensee")

## Section 1 — License Grant

Licensor grants Licensee a non-exclusive, non-transferable enterprise license
to use the Company A Analytics Platform for up to 1,000 named users. The license
includes access to all modules: Core Analytics, Predictive Modeling, and
Real-Time Dashboards.

## Section 2 — Fees and Payment

| Year | Annual License Fee | Quarterly Payment |
|------|-------------------|-------------------|
| 1    | $480,000          | $120,000          |
| 2    | $504,000          | $126,000          |
| 3    | $529,200          | $132,300          |

Total 3-year contract value: $1,513,200
Annual escalation: 5% compounding

2.1 Quarterly payments are due on the first day of each calendar quarter,
payable within 30 days of invoice.

2.2 Prepayment discount: 8% if annual fee is paid in full within 15 days of
the annual period start date.

## Section 3 — Change of Control

3.1 **Definition.** "Change of Control" means: (a) acquisition of more than
50% of voting securities, (b) merger or consolidation where existing
shareholders retain less than 50% of voting power, or (c) sale of
substantially all assets.

3.2 **Notification.** In the event of a Change of Control of Licensor,
Licensor shall notify Licensee within 10 business days of the closing of
the transaction.

3.3 **Termination Right.** Upon receiving Change of Control notice, Licensee
shall have the right to terminate this Agreement within 90 days without
penalty. Licensor shall refund all prepaid and unearned fees within 30 days
of the termination effective date.

3.4 **Service Continuity.** If Licensee elects to continue under this
Agreement following a Change of Control, the successor entity must honor
all terms, pricing, and service levels for the remainder of the current
term. Any material degradation in service quality within 12 months of the
Change of Control event shall constitute grounds for termination under
Section 3.3, with the 90-day notice period restarting.

3.5 **Anti-Assignment.** This Agreement may not be assigned by either party
without the prior written consent of the other party, which consent shall
not be unreasonably withheld. Notwithstanding the foregoing, Licensor may
assign this Agreement to a successor entity in connection with a Change of
Control, subject to the Licensee's termination right under Section 3.3.

## Section 4 — Service Level Agreement

4.1 Platform availability: 99.95% measured monthly, excluding scheduled
maintenance windows (Sundays 02:00-06:00 UTC).

4.2 Service credits for downtime beyond SLA:
- 99.90% - 99.94%: 5% of monthly fee
- 99.50% - 99.89%: 10% of monthly fee
- Below 99.50%: 25% of monthly fee

4.3 Maximum service credit per month: 25% of monthly fee.

4.4 Chronic SLA failure (3 consecutive months below 99.90%) entitles Licensee
to terminate without penalty with 30 days notice.

## Section 5 — Financial Impact Provisions

5.1 Upon termination (for any reason), Licensor shall:
(a) Refund prepaid fees for the unexpired portion of the current period,
    calculated on a pro-rata daily basis.
(b) Provide data export services at no additional charge for 60 days
    following termination.

5.2 Early termination penalty (if terminated by Licensee without cause and
not under Section 3.3 or 4.4): 25% of remaining contract value.

5.3 The total financial exposure upon a Change of Control termination under
Section 3.3 includes: prepaid fee refunds (up to $132,300 per quarter),
waiver of the early termination penalty, and 60 days of free data export.

All intellectual property in the Platform remains with Licensor. Licensee
retains ownership of all Licensee Data. Custom configurations and workflows
created by Licensee within the Platform are Licensee property.
