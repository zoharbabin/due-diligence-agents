# Managed Services Agreement — Company A / Company D

**Contract Reference:** MSA-2024-008
**Effective Date:** February 1, 2024
**Term:** 24 months
**Parties:** Company A ("Provider"), Company D ("Customer")

## Section 1 — Service Description

Provider shall deliver managed cloud infrastructure and application monitoring
services to Customer, including:
- 24/7 infrastructure monitoring and incident response
- Monthly capacity planning reviews
- Quarterly security assessments
- Automated backup and disaster recovery management

## Section 2 — Fees

| Component                | Monthly Fee  | Annual Total |
|-------------------------|-------------|-------------|
| Base managed services   | $35,000     | $420,000    |
| Premium support tier    | $8,000      | $96,000     |
| DR management           | $5,000      | $60,000     |

Total annual contract value: $576,000
Total 2-year contract value: $1,152,000

2.1 All fees are invoiced monthly in advance, due Net 30.

2.2 Annual price escalation: CPI + 1%, capped at 4%.

## Section 3 — Service Level Agreement

### 3.1 Availability SLAs

| Service Component          | SLA Target | Measurement  |
|---------------------------|-----------|-------------|
| Infrastructure monitoring | 99.99%    | Monthly      |
| Incident response (P0)   | 15 min    | Per incident |
| Incident response (P1)   | 30 min    | Per incident |
| Incident response (P2)   | 4 hours   | Per incident |
| Backup completion         | 99.95%    | Monthly      |
| DR failover test          | Quarterly | Pass/Fail    |

### 3.2 Service Credit Schedule

Failure to meet any availability SLA triggers the following credits:

**Infrastructure Monitoring:**
- 99.95% - 99.98%: 5% of monthly base fee ($1,750)
- 99.90% - 99.94%: 10% of monthly base fee ($3,500)
- 99.50% - 99.89%: 20% of monthly base fee ($7,000)
- Below 99.50%: 30% of monthly base fee ($10,500)

**Incident Response (per missed SLA):**
- Each P0 response exceeding 15 minutes: $2,500
- Each P1 response exceeding 30 minutes: $1,000
- Each P2 response exceeding 4 hours: $500

**Backup Completion:**
- Below 99.95%: 15% of DR management fee ($750)
- Below 99.00%: 50% of DR management fee ($2,500)

### 3.3 Credit Caps and Accumulation

3.3.1 Maximum aggregate service credits per calendar month: 40% of total
monthly fees ($19,200).

3.3.2 Service credits accumulate and are applied against the next invoice.
Unused credits at contract end are forfeited — no cash refund.

3.3.3 Service credits are the sole and exclusive remedy for SLA failures.
Customer waives any other claims for damages arising from SLA breaches.

### 3.4 Chronic Failure Provisions

3.4.1 If Provider fails to meet the infrastructure monitoring SLA for 3
consecutive months, Customer may terminate this Agreement with 30 days
written notice without early termination penalty.

3.4.2 If aggregate service credits exceed 25% of quarterly fees in any
quarter, Customer may request a service improvement plan. Failure to
deliver measurable improvement within 60 days triggers an additional
termination right.

## Section 4 — Financial Exposure Analysis

4.1 Maximum annual service credit liability: $230,400 (40% × $48,000 × 12).

4.2 In a worst-case scenario (all SLAs missed every month), Provider's
maximum annual credit exposure equals 40% of annual contract value.

4.3 Revenue recognition impact: service credits reduce recognized revenue
in the period they are incurred. Provider must accrue estimated credits
based on trailing 3-month SLA performance.

4.4 The interaction between service credits and revenue recognition creates
a compounding effect: months with poor SLA performance reduce both current
revenue (via credits) and future revenue projections (via accrual adjustments).

## Section 5 — Term and Termination

5.1 Initial term: 24 months. Auto-renewal for successive 12-month periods
unless either party provides 90 days written notice.

5.2 Early termination by Customer (without cause): 50% of remaining
contract value as termination fee.

5.3 Termination for cause: 30 days notice with opportunity to cure.

5.4 Upon termination, Provider shall assist with migration for up to 90
days at standard hourly rates ($275/hour).

## Section 6 — Limitation of Liability

6.1 Provider's aggregate liability under this Agreement shall not exceed
the total fees paid by Customer in the 12 months preceding the claim.

6.2 Neither party shall be liable for indirect, consequential, or
punitive damages.

6.3 Exclusions from liability cap: willful misconduct, breach of
confidentiality, data breach resulting from Provider negligence.

## Section 7 — Governing Law

Laws of the State of Texas. Disputes resolved by binding arbitration
under AAA Commercial Arbitration Rules, seated in Austin, Texas.
