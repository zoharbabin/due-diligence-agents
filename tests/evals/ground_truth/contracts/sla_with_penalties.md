# Service Level Agreement

**Between:** Company A ("Provider") and Company B ("Client")
**Effective Date:** February 1, 2024
**Related Agreement:** Master Services Agreement dated January 1, 2024

## Section 1 — Availability

1.1 Provider guarantees 99.9% monthly uptime for all production systems.

1.2 Scheduled maintenance windows (Sundays 02:00-06:00 UTC) are excluded
from uptime calculations.

## Section 2 — Service Credits

2.1 If monthly uptime falls below 99.9%, the following credits apply:

| Uptime          | Service Credit    |
|----------------|-------------------|
| 99.0% - 99.9%  | 10% of monthly fee |
| 95.0% - 99.0%  | 25% of monthly fee |
| Below 95.0%    | 50% of monthly fee |

2.2 Service credits are capped at 50% of the monthly fee in any given month.

2.3 Client must request credits within 30 days of the incident.

## Section 3 — Response Times

| Severity   | Response Time | Resolution Target |
|-----------|--------------|-------------------|
| Critical   | 15 minutes   | 4 hours           |
| High       | 1 hour       | 8 hours           |
| Medium     | 4 hours      | 2 business days   |
| Low        | 1 business day | 5 business days  |

## Section 4 — Penalty Escalation

4.1 If Provider fails to meet the Critical response time SLA for three or more
incidents in any calendar quarter, Client may terminate the MSA with 30 days
notice.

4.2 Repeated SLA breaches (more than 5 in any 6-month period) trigger an
automatic 15% fee reduction for the following 6-month period.

## Section 5 — Reporting

Provider shall deliver monthly SLA compliance reports within 10 business days
of each month-end.

## Section 6 — Exclusions

SLA commitments do not apply during force majeure events or when downtime is
caused by Client's equipment or actions.
