# Intellectual Property Assignment Agreement

**Between:** Company A ("Assignor") and Company B ("Assignee")
**Effective Date:** March 15, 2024

## Section 1 — Assignment

1.1 Assignor hereby assigns to Assignee all right, title, and interest in and
to the software known as "Project Alpha" (the "Work Product"), including all
intellectual property rights therein.

1.2 Notwithstanding Section 1.1, Assignor retains a non-exclusive, perpetual,
royalty-free license to use the Work Product for internal research purposes.

## Section 2 — Representations

2.1 Assignor represents that it is the sole owner of the Work Product and has
not granted any licenses, liens, or encumbrances on the Work Product, except
as disclosed in Exhibit B.

2.2 Assignor represents that no third-party open-source components are
incorporated into the Work Product. (Note: Exhibit B is not attached.)

## Section 3 — Consideration

Assignee shall pay Assignor $250,000 as full consideration for the assignment.

## Section 4 — Further Assurances

Assignor agrees to execute any additional documents reasonably necessary to
perfect the assignment contemplated herein.

## Section 5 — Governing Law

This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of California.
