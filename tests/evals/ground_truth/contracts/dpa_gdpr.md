# Data Processing Agreement

**Between:** Company A ("Processor") and Company B ("Controller")
**Effective Date:** April 1, 2024

## Section 1 — Scope of Processing

1.1 Processor shall process personal data solely on behalf of Controller and
only in accordance with Controller's documented instructions.

1.2 Categories of data subjects: employees, customers, and prospective
customers of Controller.

1.3 Types of personal data: names, email addresses, IP addresses, usage
analytics, and payment information.

## Section 2 — Sub-processors

2.1 Processor currently engages the following sub-processors:
- Cloud Host Co (infrastructure hosting, EU region)
- Analytics Platform Inc (anonymized usage analytics, US region)

2.2 Processor shall notify Controller at least 30 days before engaging any new
sub-processor. Controller may object within 14 days.

## Section 3 — Data Transfers

3.1 Personal data shall be stored and processed within the European Economic
Area (EEA).

3.2 Notwithstanding Section 3.1, Analytics Platform Inc processes anonymized
analytics data in the United States. Processor asserts that anonymization
removes all personal data identifiers.

3.3 No Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) or adequacy decision is referenced
for the US transfer described in Section 3.2.

## Section 4 — Security Measures

4.1 Processor shall implement appropriate technical and organizational measures
including encryption at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.2+).

4.2 Processor shall conduct annual penetration testing and share summary
results with Controller upon request.

## Section 5 — Breach Notification

5.1 Processor shall notify Controller of any personal data breach within
72 hours of becoming aware.

5.2 Notification shall include the nature of the breach, categories of data
affected, and remediation steps taken.

## Section 6 — Data Subject Rights

Processor shall assist Controller in fulfilling data subject requests (access,
rectification, erasure, portability) within 15 business days.

## Section 7 — Term and Deletion

7.1 This DPA remains in effect for the duration of the underlying MSA.

7.2 Upon termination, Processor shall delete all personal data within 60 days
unless retention is required by applicable law.

## Section 8 — Governing Law

This DPA is governed by the laws of the Federal Republic of Germany.
