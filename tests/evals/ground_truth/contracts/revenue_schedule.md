# Revenue and Payment Schedule — Company A

**Contract Reference:** Service Agreement SA-2024-001
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024
**Client:** Company B

## Section 1 — Fee Structure

| Period       | Quarterly Fee | Annual Total |
|-------------|--------------|-------------|
| Year 1      | $125,000     | $500,000    |
| Year 2      | $137,500     | $550,000    |
| Year 3      | $150,000     | $600,000    |

Total contract value: $1,650,000 over 3 years.

## Section 2 — Payment Terms

2.1 All invoices are due Net 45 from date of invoice.

2.2 Late payments accrue interest at 1.5% per month (18% per annum).

## Section 3 — Revenue Recognition

3.1 Revenue shall be recognized ratably over each quarterly service period.

3.2 Upfront implementation fees of $75,000 are recognized upon completion of
implementation milestones as defined in Exhibit C.

3.3 Note: The $75,000 implementation fee is not reflected in the fee table
above, bringing the true total contract value to $1,725,000.

## Section 4 — Early Termination

4.1 If Client terminates before the end of Year 1, Client shall pay a
termination fee equal to 50% of remaining Year 1 fees.

4.2 After Year 1, no early termination fee applies.

## Section 5 — Discounts

5.1 A 5% discount applies if Client pays the full annual amount within 15 days
of invoice date. This discount is not reflected in the fee table above.
