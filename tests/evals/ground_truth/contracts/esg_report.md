# Environmental, Social, and Governance Report

**Entity:** Company A
**Reporting Period:** FY2023
**Date:** January 30, 2024

## Section 1 — Environmental

1.1 Total Scope 1 emissions: 120 tonnes CO2e (on-site generators, company vehicles).

1.2 Total Scope 2 emissions: 890 tonnes CO2e (electricity consumption across
3 office locations). No renewable energy certificates (RECs) have been purchased.

1.3 Scope 3 emissions have not been measured. The company has not committed to
Science Based Targets initiative (SBTi) or any net-zero timeline.

1.4 Data center operations (co-located) consume approximately 2.1 GWh annually.
The hosting provider reports 40% renewable energy sourcing, but this is not
independently verified.

## Section 2 — Social

2.1 Total workforce: 312 employees across 4 countries.

2.2 Gender diversity: 28% of engineering roles and 33% of management roles
are held by women. The board has 1 female director out of 5 total.

2.3 Employee turnover for FY2023: 22%, above the industry median of 16%.
Exit interviews cite compensation competitiveness and limited career
progression as primary factors.

2.4 No formal Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion (DEI) program is in place.
An employee resource group was formed in Q3 2023 but operates without
dedicated budget.

2.5 Supply chain labor standards: the company has no formal supplier code of
conduct. Key suppliers have not been audited for labor practices.

## Section 3 — Governance

3.1 Board composition: 5 directors, 2 independent. This does not meet the
majority-independent standard recommended by institutional investors.

3.2 The company has a Code of Ethics adopted in 2020, last reviewed in 2022.
No whistleblower hotline exists.

3.3 Executive compensation is not tied to ESG metrics. Annual bonuses are
based solely on revenue and EBITDA targets.

3.4 No ESG committee exists at the board level. ESG matters are reviewed
by the Audit Committee on an ad-hoc basis.

## Section 4 — Reporting and Frameworks

4.1 The company does not report against any recognized ESG framework (GRI,
SASB, TCFD, or CDP).

4.2 No third-party ESG rating or assessment has been obtained.

4.3 Material ESG risks have not been integrated into the enterprise risk
management (ERM) framework.
