# Professional Services Agreement

**Between:** Company A ("Consultant") and Company B ("Client")
**Effective Date:** June 1, 2024
**Term:** 24 months

## Section 1 — Services

Consultant shall provide strategic advisory services related to digital
transformation as outlined in the Statement of Work attached as Exhibit A.

## Section 2 — Fees

Client shall pay Consultant $200,000 per year, invoiced monthly in arrears.

## Section 3 — Key Personnel

3.1 Consultant agrees that the following individuals are essential to the
performance of this Agreement: the Lead Partner, Senior Analyst, and Technical
Architect (collectively, "Key Personnel").

3.2 Any material change in the management structure, ownership composition, or
operational control of Consultant that results in the departure or reassignment
of Key Personnel shall be considered a material event requiring Client
notification within 10 business days.

3.3 Client reserves the right to assess whether any such material event
adversely impacts the quality or continuity of services, and may request a
remediation plan within 30 days of notification.

## Section 4 — Termination

4.1 Either party may terminate this Agreement for convenience upon 60 days
written notice.

4.2 Either party may terminate for cause if the other party commits a material
breach that remains uncured after 30 days written notice.

4.3 In the event of a fundamental restructuring of Consultant's business —
including but not limited to merger with a competitor of Client, transfer of
substantially all assets, or change in primary business purpose — Client may,
at its sole discretion, elect to discontinue the engagement upon 15 days notice.

## Section 5 — Confidentiality

Both parties agree to maintain confidentiality of proprietary information
shared during the engagement for a period of 3 years following termination.

## Section 6 — Governing Law

This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of New York.
