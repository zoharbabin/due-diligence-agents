# Revenue and Payment Schedule — Company A

**Contract Reference:** License Agreement LA-2024-003
**Effective Date:** March 1, 2024
**Licensee:** Company B

## Section 1 — License Fee Structure

| Period       | Quarterly Fee | Annual Total |
|-------------|--------------|-------------|
| Year 1      | $75,000      | $300,000    |
| Year 2      | $82,500      | $330,000    |
| Year 3      | $90,000      | $360,000    |

Total contract value: $990,000 over 3 years.

## Section 2 — Payment Terms

2.1 All invoices are due Net 30 from date of invoice.

2.2 Late payments accrue interest at 2.0% per month (24% per annum).

## Section 3 — Revenue Recognition

3.1 License revenue shall be recognized ratably over each quarterly period.

3.2 A one-time setup fee of $50,000 is payable upon execution. This fee
covers implementation, training, and initial configuration.

3.3 The $50,000 setup fee is not included in the fee table above, bringing
the true total contract value to $1,040,000.

## Section 4 — Audit Rights

4.1 Licensor reserves the right to audit Licensee's usage of the software
annually. Licensee shall provide reasonable access to usage logs and records.

4.2 If an audit reveals underpayment exceeding 5%, Licensee shall pay the
shortfall plus a 10% penalty within 30 days.

## Section 5 — Special Provisions

5.1 In the event that Licensor undergoes a change of control (defined as
acquisition of more than 50% of voting shares, merger, or sale of
substantially all assets), Licensee shall have the option to:

(a) Continue under this agreement with the successor entity; or
(b) Terminate this agreement with 90 days written notice and receive a
    pro-rata refund of prepaid fees.

5.2 This termination right under Section 5.1(b) expires 120 days after
Licensee receives written notice of the change of control event.

## Section 6 — Discount and Incentive Terms

6.1 Early payment discount: 3% if full quarterly payment is received
within 10 days of invoice.

6.2 Volume incentive: If Licensee's usage exceeds 500 named users, the
per-user rate for Year 3 drops by 12%. This discount is not reflected
in the fee table.
