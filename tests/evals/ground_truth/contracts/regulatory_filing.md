# Regulatory Compliance Summary

**Entity:** Company A
**Prepared by:** Compliance Department
**Date:** February 15, 2024

## Section 1 — Licenses and Permits

1.1 Company A holds a Payment Facilitator license issued by the State of New
York Department of Financial Services, renewed annually. Current license
expires December 31, 2024.

1.2 The Ontario subsidiary is registered as an Information Technology Service
Provider with the Ontario Securities Commission (OSC) under NI 31-103.

1.3 No license or registration is held in the EU for payment services.
Management is evaluating whether PSD2 registration is required following
expansion into the German market.

## Section 2 — Regulatory Filings

2.1 Annual compliance reports have been filed on time for FY2021 and FY2022.
The FY2023 filing is due by March 31, 2024.

2.2 The entity is subject to anti-money laundering (AML) requirements under
FinCEN. The most recent AML audit was completed in June 2023 with no
material findings.

2.3 Know Your Customer (KYC) procedures are documented but have not been
independently verified since 2021.

## Section 3 — Enforcement Actions

3.1 In September 2023, the NY DFS issued a consent order requiring Company A
to remediate deficiencies in its transaction monitoring systems within 180
days. The remediation deadline is March 2024.

3.2 No other enforcement actions, fines, or penalties are pending or have been
issued in the past 3 years.

## Section 4 — Industry-Specific Requirements

4.1 PCI DSS Level 1 certification is maintained and was last audited in
October 2023. No critical findings.

4.2 Company A is not currently subject to CFPB oversight but may trigger
the threshold (10,000 annual transactions) in Q2 2024 based on growth
projections.

## Section 5 — Change of Control Implications

5.1 The NY DFS Payment Facilitator license requires prior written approval
for any change of control. Processing time is typically 90-120 days.

5.2 The OSC registration may require amendment notification within 30 days
of a change of control event.

5.3 PCI DSS certification is entity-specific and does not transfer
automatically upon acquisition. A re-certification assessment may be required.
