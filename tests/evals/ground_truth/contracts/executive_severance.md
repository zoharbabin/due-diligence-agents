# Executive Employment Agreement

**Between:** Company A ("Employer") and Robert Chen ("Executive")
**Effective Date:** March 1, 2024
**Position:** Chief Revenue Officer

## Section 1 — Compensation

1.1 Base salary: USD 340,000 per annum, paid semi-monthly.

1.2 Annual target bonus: 60% of base salary, with a guaranteed minimum of 50%
for the first contract year.

1.3 Long-term incentive: 120,000 restricted stock units (RSUs) vesting over
4 years, 25% per year, with a 1-year cliff.

1.4 Signing bonus: USD 75,000, repayable in full if the Executive resigns
without good reason within 24 months.

## Section 2 — Termination

2.1 The Employer may terminate the Executive without cause upon 30 days written
notice, subject to the severance provisions below.

2.2 Upon termination without cause, the Executive is entitled to 18 months of
base salary continuation, full target bonus for the year of termination, and
12 months of continued health benefits.

2.3 Termination for cause (defined as fraud, willful misconduct, or material
breach) results in forfeiture of all unvested RSUs and no severance.

## Section 3 — Change of Control

3.1 Upon a change of control, all unvested RSUs accelerate and vest in full.

3.2 If the Executive is terminated without cause or resigns for good reason
within 18 months following a change of control, the Executive receives a lump
sum equal to 2x base salary plus 2x target bonus (double-trigger).

## Section 4 — Restrictive Covenants

4.1 For 12 months following termination, the Executive shall not engage in any
competing business in North America or solicit Employer's customers or
employees.

4.2 The non-compete is expressly acknowledged by the Executive as reasonable
and enforceable as a condition of the equity grant.

## Section 5 — Governing Law

This agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Delaware, USA.
