# Consulting Services Agreement — Company A / Company B

**Contract Reference:** CSA-2024-017
**Effective Date:** January 15, 2024
**Parties:** Company A ("Provider"), Company B ("Client")

## Section 1 — Scope of Services

Provider shall deliver data analytics consulting services to Client, including:
- Data pipeline architecture and implementation
- Custom dashboard development
- Quarterly business intelligence reviews

## Section 2 — Fee Structure

| Service Component       | Fee Type      | Amount     |
|------------------------|---------------|------------|
| Monthly retainer       | Recurring     | $45,000    |
| Implementation fee     | One-time      | $120,000   |
| Success bonus          | Milestone     | $75,000    |

Annual recurring value: $540,000
Total Year 1 contract value: $735,000

## Section 3 — Revenue Recognition Provisions

3.1 The monthly retainer of $45,000 shall be invoiced on the first business day
of each month and is due Net 30. Revenue is recognized upon delivery of the
monthly service report.

3.2 The implementation fee of $120,000 is payable 50% upon contract execution
and 50% upon completion of Phase 2 milestones. Provider recognizes revenue for
the implementation fee ratably over the 6-month implementation period, regardless
of payment timing.

3.3 The success bonus of $75,000 is contingent upon Client achieving a 15%
improvement in data processing efficiency, as measured by the KPI framework
defined in Exhibit B. Provider shall recognize the success bonus only upon
formal written confirmation from Client that the KPI threshold has been met.

3.4 NOTE: The total contract value stated in Section 2 ($735,000) does not
include potential overage charges for additional consulting hours beyond the
monthly retainer scope, billed at $350/hour.

## Section 4 — Delivery and Acceptance

4.1 Each deliverable under this Agreement is subject to Client acceptance.
Client shall have 15 business days to review and accept or reject each
deliverable. Rejection must include specific deficiency details.

4.2 If Client fails to respond within the 15 business day acceptance window,
the deliverable shall be deemed accepted.

4.3 Provider shall have 10 business days to remediate rejected deliverables.
If remediation fails after two attempts, Client may terminate the affected
SOW without further obligation for that SOW's remaining fees.

## Section 5 — Refund and Clawback Provisions

5.1 If Provider fails to deliver the implementation milestones by the agreed
timeline (Exhibit A), Client is entitled to a refund of 25% of the
implementation fee paid to date.

5.2 If the KPI targets in Exhibit B are not achieved within 12 months of
go-live, Provider shall refund 50% of the success bonus if previously paid,
or waive the bonus if not yet invoiced.

5.3 Monthly retainer fees are non-refundable once the monthly service report
has been delivered and accepted (or deemed accepted per Section 4.2).

## Section 6 — Term and Termination

6.1 Initial term: 24 months from the Effective Date.

6.2 Either party may terminate for material breach with 30 days written notice
and opportunity to cure.

6.3 Client may terminate for convenience with 90 days written notice. Upon
termination for convenience, Client shall pay: (a) all fees for services
rendered through the termination date, (b) 50% of the remaining monthly
retainer fees for the unexpired term, and (c) any earned but unpaid success
bonus.

## Section 7 — Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of New York.
Disputes shall be resolved by binding arbitration under JAMS rules.
