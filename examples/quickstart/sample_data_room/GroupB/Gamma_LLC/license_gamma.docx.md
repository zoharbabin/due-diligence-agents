# Enterprise License Agreement

**Between:** Gamma Group LLC ("Customer") and NovaBridge Solutions, Inc. ("Vendor")

**Effective Date:** March 10, 2025
**Agreement Number:** ELA-2025-00067

---

## Recitals

WHEREAS, Vendor develops and operates a cloud-based supply-chain analytics platform; and

WHEREAS, Customer desires to license and use said platform for its internal operations across its North American business units;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants herein, the parties agree as follows:

## 1. Scope of License

Vendor grants Customer a non-exclusive, worldwide license to use the NovaBridge Analytics Platform ("Platform") during the Term, subject to the following parameters:

- **Named Users:** Up to 75
- **Concurrent Sessions:** Up to 30
- **Modules:** Core Analytics, Predictive Forecasting, Supply Chain Optimizer
- **Data Retention:** 36 months rolling
- **Environments:** Production + 1 non-production (staging)

## 2. Term

The initial term shall be twelve (12) months from the Effective Date ("Initial Term"). This Agreement shall renew automatically for successive twelve (12) month periods unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal at least forty-five (45) days before the end of the then-current term.

## 3. Fees and Payment

| Component | Amount | Frequency |
|---|---|---|
| Platform License (75 users) | $112,500.00 | Annual |
| Predictive Forecasting Module | $18,000.00 | Annual |
| Supply Chain Optimizer Module | $22,500.00 | Annual |
| Premium Onboarding Package | $15,000.00 | One-time |
| **Year 1 Total** | **$168,000.00** | |
| **Annual Recurring (Year 2+)** | **$153,000.00** | |

All fees are exclusive of applicable taxes. Payment is due within thirty (30) days of invoice date.

## 4. Service Levels

Vendor shall maintain Platform availability of no less than 99.5% measured monthly, excluding scheduled maintenance windows (Sundays 02:00-06:00 UTC). For each full percentage point below the SLA threshold in a given month, Customer shall receive a service credit equal to 5% of the monthly license fee, up to a maximum credit of 20% per month.

## 5. Data Security and Compliance

Vendor shall maintain SOC 2 Type II certification and comply with all applicable data protection laws. Customer data shall be stored in AWS US-East-1 region unless otherwise agreed. Vendor shall notify Customer of any data breach within 48 hours of discovery.

## 6. Change of Control

In the event of a Change of Control of Vendor (defined as any transaction resulting in a change of more than 50% of Vendor's voting securities or a sale of substantially all assets), Vendor shall provide written notice to Customer within fifteen (15) business days. Customer shall have the right to terminate this Agreement without penalty within ninety (90) days of receiving such notice, and shall receive a pro-rata refund of any prepaid fees.

## 7. Limitation of Liability

Vendor's total aggregate liability shall not exceed the fees paid by Customer in the twelve (12) months preceding the claim. Neither party shall be liable for indirect, special, incidental, or consequential damages.

## 8. Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of New York.
